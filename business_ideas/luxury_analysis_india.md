| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Affordable PCs & High-End GPUs | 4,500 | **Business Problem** | **Hardware Import Taxes:** High-end electronics are heavily taxed in India. **Idea:** Affordable, low-latency Cloud Gaming and Cloud-PC subscription services tailored for Indian students and gamers on local servers. |
| **2** | Software without Monthly Subscriptions | 3,600 | **Business Problem** | **Subscription Fatigue:** Users resent renting basic tools (like Adobe or weather apps). **Idea:** Build lifetime-license, localized SaaS alternatives specifically targeted at Indian freelancers and SMBs. |
| **3** | Functional Search Engine Results | 3,200 | **Business Problem** | **SEO/AI Saturation:** Hard to find local, authentic information. **Idea:** A hyper-local, human-curated discovery platform for Indian cities that filters out AI fluff and heavy affiliate marketing. |
| **4** | 24-Hour Stores and Restaurants | 1,000 | **Readily Available** | India leads in this space with "Quick Commerce" (Zepto, Blinkit, Swiggy Instamart), which fills the 24/7 void left by physical retail. |
| **5** | Guilt-Free Free Time & Relaxing | 969 | **Business Problem** | **Hustle Culture:** Severe productivity pressure in metro hubs. **Idea:** "Tech-Detox Retreats" near major IT corridors (Bengaluru, Pune) where devices are locked away, and "idleness" is the core service. |
| **6** | Boring / Predictable Politics | 900 | **Experience** | Not a traditional business problem, but highlights a demand for "News Sanitization" apps that provide only objective, un-sensationalized policy updates. |
| **7** | Affordable "Diner" Breakfasts | 562 | **Business Problem** | **The Third Space Premium:** Cafes are expensive, and street food hygiene varies. **Idea:** Modernize the "Darshini" or Irani Cafe model—highly standardized, hygienic, tech-enabled QSRs that bridge the gap between street stalls and premium cafes. |
| **8** | Good Coffee / $6 Espresso | 467 | **Readily Available** | Specialty coffee is booming in India (Blue Tokai, Third Wave), but remains a premium luxury rather than a cheap staple. |
| **9** | Affordable Meat & Groceries | 455 | **Business Problem** | **Protein Inflation:** Quality meat is getting expensive. **Idea:** Direct-to-consumer farm aggregators offering "Meat Subscription Boxes" to cut out middlemen and stabilize weekly household costs. |
| **10** | Solid Wood Furniture | 212 | **Readily Available** | Unlike the West, local Indian carpentry still offers solid wood as a standard, though timber prices are rising. |
| **11** | Weekend Mountain Biking / Travel | 161 | **Business Problem** | **Weekend Inflation:** Resort stays are extremely pricey. **Idea:** Micro-stay "adventure pods" or self-service container homes on the outskirts of cities for cheap, frictionless weekend escapes. |
| **12** | Having a Stable Job / Easy Hiring | 105 | **Business Problem** | **The Skill Gap:** High competition and layoffs. **Idea:** Outcome-based, micro-credentialing platforms that partner directly with Indian enterprises to train candidates for specific, immediate roles. |
| **13** | Doing Things Without a Smartphone | 95 | **Business Problem** | **Digital Dependency:** India runs on UPI; you need a phone. **Idea:** The "Premium Dumbphone"—a sleek, distraction-free device that *only* supports UPI payments, WhatsApp, and Maps, aimed at students prepping for UPSC/JEE or professionals needing focus. |
| **14** | Privacy / Living Without Roommates | 62 | **Business Problem** | **Urban Density:** Severe in places like Mumbai and Bengaluru. **Idea:** Affordable, modular acoustic panels or soundproof "work pods" for shared apartments to give a luxury sense of privacy. |
| **15** | Cheap Fast Food | 62 | **Readily Available** | Street food remains affordable, though Western fast-food chains are now considered premium dining for many. |
| **16** | Affordable Jeans / Clothing | 49 | **Readily Available** | Easily available via local markets, D2C brands, and platforms like Myntra or Ajio. |
| **17** | Affordable Used Cars ("Beaters") | 20 | **Business Problem** | **Asset Inflation:** Used car prices have skyrocketed. **Idea:** "Long-Term Beater Leases"—a service renting older, reliable hatchbacks with maintenance included for a low, flat monthly fee for daily commutes. |
